Module Description: 								
	DJTG Demo demonstrates how to read in IDCODEs from the		
	JTAG scan chain. Codes for some Digilent FPGA boards are
	given below.									

	Nexys2: 0x41c22093			
			0xf5046093				
			
	Basys2: 0x11c10093		
			0xf5045093	


Hardware Setup:
	Connect any board that supports DJTG, such as the Nexys2 or 
	Basys2, via USB.
